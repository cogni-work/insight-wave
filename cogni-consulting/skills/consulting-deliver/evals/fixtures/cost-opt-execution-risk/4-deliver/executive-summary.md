# Executive Summary

## Recommendation

**Conditional Go: Hybrid Optimization + Transformation**

RegioBank should pursue a combined cloud optimization and infrastructure consolidation program targeting €2.0M annual savings (63% of the €3.2M target). The remaining savings require vendor contract renegotiation (post-Q3 2027) and are recommended as Phase 2.

## Context

RegioBank operates IT at 8.2% of revenue versus a 6-7% industry average, with €16M annual costs across underutilized data centers (35% utilization), unmanaged cloud spend (growing 30% YoY), and an outsourced support model where 80% of Tier 1 tickets are automatable. The 12-month timeline and no-headcount-reduction policy constrain the approach.

## Key Findings

- **3 options evaluated**: Cloud Quick Wins (safe but insufficient at €1M), Hybrid (recommended at €2M), Full Transformation (highest savings but exceeds timeline)
- **16 claims verified**: 13 confirmed, 2 adjusted downward (AWS savings and DC2 costs were both overstated by ~20%), 1 range estimate
- **Business case robust**: Positive NPV even in pessimistic scenario (€2.2M over 3 years)

## Conditions for Approval

1. BaFin pre-submission consultation before DC2 cloud migration
2. Works council alignment on support model changes
3. FinOps governance established before counting AWS savings

## Next Steps

1. **Week 1**: Launch AWS rightsizing (immediate cost reduction)
2. **Week 2**: Initiate BaFin pre-consultation on cloud DR
3. **Month 1**: FinOps governance framework and tooling selection
4. **Month 3**: Gate decision — proceed to DC2 migration based on BaFin response

## Risks

- **BaFin additional requirements** (Medium): Could delay DC2 migration 2-3 months
- **Self-service adoption** (Medium): Lower-than-expected adoption reduces savings by €70-140K
- **Staff retraining resistance** (Medium): Mitigated by early change communication and no-layoff commitment
