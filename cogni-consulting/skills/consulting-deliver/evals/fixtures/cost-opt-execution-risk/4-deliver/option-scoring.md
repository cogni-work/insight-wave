# Option Scoring

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Savings magnitude | 25% | Must approach or exceed the 20% (€3.2M) target |
| Timeline fit | 25% | Must deliver within 12 months |
| Implementation risk | 20% | Likelihood of successful execution given constraints |
| Sustainability | 15% | Are savings structural or one-time? |
| Staff impact | 15% | Alignment with no-headcount-reduction policy + retraining feasibility |

## Scoring Matrix

| Option | Savings (25%) | Timeline (25%) | Risk (20%) | Sustainability (15%) | Staff Impact (15%) | **Total** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| A: Cloud Quick Wins | 2 | 5 | 5 | 2 | 5 | **3.7** |
| B: Hybrid Optimization | 4 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 4 | **3.8** |
| C: Full Transformation | 5 | 2 | 2 | 5 | 3 | **3.3** |

## Ranking

1. **Option B: Hybrid Optimization + Transformation** (3.8/5) — Recommended
2. **Option A: Cloud-First Quick Wins** (3.7/5) — Safe but insufficient
3. **Option C: Full Structural Transformation** (3.3/5) — Most impactful but highest risk

## Discussion

Option B wins by a narrow margin over Option A, primarily due to higher savings and sustainability scores. The 0.1-point margin reflects real tension between safety (A) and ambition (B). The tie-breaking factor is sustainability — cloud-only savings erode as usage grows, while data center consolidation and support model changes create structural improvement.

The scoring includes "Staff Impact" at 15% to reflect the no-headcount-reduction constraint and the works council dynamics identified in Discovery.
