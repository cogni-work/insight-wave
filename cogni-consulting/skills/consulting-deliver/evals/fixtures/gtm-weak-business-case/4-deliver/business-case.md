# Business Case: DiGA-First Sequential

## Investment Summary

| Item | Amount | Period |
|---|---|---|
| CE marking completion + DiGA submission | €180K | Months 1-6 |
| Real-world evidence study (observational) | €150K | Months 3-12 |
| Commercial team operations (4 FTE) | €320K | Months 1-18 |
| Distribution partner development | €80K | Months 6-14 |
| Marketing & market access | €70K | Months 10-18 |
| **Total investment** | **€800K** | **18 months** |

## Expected Revenue

HealthBridge targets 200 patients in year 1 post-DiGA approval, scaling to 800 patients by end of year 2.

| Revenue Stream | Year 1 | Year 2 | Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| DiGA reimbursement (€600/patient/quarter) | €480K | €1,920K | 200 patients × €600 × 4 quarters (Year 1); 800 patients × €600 × 4 quarters (Year 2) |
| Platform licensing (B2B) | €50K | €200K | 2 clinic partnerships Year 1, 6 Year 2 |
| **Total revenue** | **€530K** | **€2,120K** | |

## Key Financial Metrics

- **Break-even**: Month 20 (from project start)
- **Cumulative revenue (24 months)**: €2,650K
- **ROI (24 months)**: 231%

## Key Assumptions

| Assumption | Status |
|---|---|
| European RPM market: €4.2B by 2028 | Verified (Grand View Research) |
| DiGA reimbursement: €600/patient/quarter | Based on comparable DiGA listings |
| 200 patients in year 1 via distribution partner | Industry benchmark |
| Distribution partner secured by month 10 | Planned |
| DiGA approval within 14 months | Based on BfArM published timelines |
| HealthBridge AI outperforms competitor monitoring-only solutions | Product architecture advantage |

## Risks

- DiGA approval delay beyond 14 months
- Distribution partner negotiations take longer than expected
- Competitor launches similar AI-enhanced RPM during our approval period
- UK DTAC pathway remains uncertain, limiting secondary market

## Recommendation

**Go** — Proceed with DiGA-First Sequential approach. The market opportunity is substantial (€4.2B European RPM market), HealthBridge has a clear differentiator (AI clinical decision support), and the DiGA pathway provides a defined route to reimbursed revenue. Speed is essential given the 18-month runway.
