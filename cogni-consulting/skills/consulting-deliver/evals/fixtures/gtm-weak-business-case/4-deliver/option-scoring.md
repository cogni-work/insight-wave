# Option Scoring

| Criterion | Weight | Rationale |

## Ranking

1. **Option A: DiGA-First Sequential** (3.5/5) — Recommended
2. **Option C: Evidence-First Bridge** (3.2/5) — Strategically strongest but exceeds runway
3. **Option B: Parallel Market Entry** (2.8/5) — Spreads resources too thin

## Discussion

Option A wins primarily on revenue timeline — it is the only option that plausibly delivers revenue before the 18-month runway expires. The margin over Option C (0.3 points) is narrow, driven entirely by the funding constraint. If HealthBridge secures bridge funding, Option C becomes the stronger choice.

Option B scores lowest because it requires splitting a 4-person commercial team across two regulatory jurisdictions, and UK DTAC uncertainty post-2025 reform makes the parallel investment risky.
