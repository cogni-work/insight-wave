# Action Roadmap

## Phase Overview

| Phase | Timeline | Focus | Budget |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1: Regulatory & Evidence | Months 1-8 | CE marking, DiGA submission, observational study start | €330K |
| 2: Commercial Preparation | Months 6-14 | Distribution partner, market access, pricing | €230K |
| 3: Market Entry | Months 12-18 | DiGA approval, first patients, scale | €240K |

## Phase 1: Regulatory & Evidence (Months 1-8)

**Goal**: Complete CE marking, submit DiGA application, start real-world evidence study

### Workstreams
- Complete CE marking process (expected Q2 2026)
- Prepare DiGA application package (clinical evaluation, data protection, interoperability)
- Launch observational study with 1-2 partner clinics (50 patients target)
- Initiate BfArM pre-submission meeting

### Decision Gate → Phase 2
- CE marking obtained
- DiGA application submitted to BfArM
- Observational study enrolled ≥30 patients

## Phase 2: Commercial Preparation (Months 6-14)

**Goal**: Secure distribution partner, establish pricing, prepare market access

### Workstreams
- Identify and negotiate with MedTech distributors (target: signed LOI by month 10)
- Develop pricing strategy for provisional DiGA listing
- Build KOL relationships in target therapeutic areas
- Prepare market access dossier for Krankenkassen

### Decision Gate → Phase 3
- Distribution partner LOI signed
- Provisional DiGA listing pricing agreed
- ≥2 KOL endorsements secured

## Phase 3: Market Entry (Months 12-18)

**Goal**: Achieve DiGA approval, onboard first patients, demonstrate traction for Series A

### Workstreams
- Support BfArM evaluation process
- Activate distribution partner network
- Onboard first 50 patients through partner clinics
- Scale to 200 patients by month 18
- Prepare Series A materials with traction data

### Decision Gate → Series A
- DiGA provisional listing obtained
- ≥100 patients on platform
- Revenue run-rate demonstrable for investor conversations
