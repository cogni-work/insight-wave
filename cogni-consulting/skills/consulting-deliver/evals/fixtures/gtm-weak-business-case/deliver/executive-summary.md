# Executive Summary

## Recommendation

**Go: DiGA-First Sequential Approach**

HealthBridge should focus all resources on the German DiGA pathway as the fastest route to reimbursed revenue. The European remote patient monitoring market is growing to €4.2B by 2028, and HealthBridge's AI-enhanced platform is well-positioned to capture share through the DiGA reimbursement channel.

## Context

HealthBridge has developed a remote patient monitoring platform with integrated AI clinical decision support — a differentiated product in a fragmented market. With CE marking expected in Q2 2026 and an 18-month funding runway, the company needs to achieve first paying customers before funding runs out.

## Key Findings

- **3 GTM options evaluated**: DiGA-First (recommended), Parallel UK+DE (too resource-intensive), Evidence-First (exceeds runway)
- **Market opportunity**: €4.2B European RPM market by 2028, with DiGA providing reimbursement of up to €600/patient/quarter
- **Differentiation**: AI-driven clinical decision support vs. monitoring-only competitors
- **11 claims verified**: 5 confirmed, 3 deviated, 3 sources unavailable
