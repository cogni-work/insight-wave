# Desk Research: IT Cost Optimization in Financial Services

## Key Findings

### 1. Cloud Migration as Cost Lever
Financial services firms migrating to cloud report 15-25% infrastructure cost reduction after 24 months. However, initial 12-month costs often increase 10-20% due to dual-running. Regulatory constraints (BaFin, EBA) limit public cloud adoption for core banking — hybrid cloud is the pragmatic path.

### 2. Automation Opportunity
IT operations automation (AIOps, runbook automation) can reduce incident response time by 40-60% and free 15-25% of ops staff capacity for higher-value work. Adoption in German Mittelstand banking is low (<15%).
