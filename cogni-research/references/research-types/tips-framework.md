# TIPS Framework Definition

## Purpose

TIPS is a strategic foresight synthesis structure that transforms research findings into actionable intelligence through four progressive components.

---

## Structure Definition

| Component | Short Name | Guiding Question |
|-----------|------------|------------------|
| **T**rend | Signal | "What is happening?" |
| **I**mplications | Impact | "What does it mean?" |
| **P**ossibilities | Options | "What could we do?" |
| **S**olutions | Action | "What should we do?" |

---

## Component Definitions

### Trend (T) — Signal

**Purpose:** Establish observable pattern grounded in evidence

**Content Focus:**
- Data-backed observations (numbers, dates, growth rates)
- Adoption curves and market statistics
- Temporal patterns (YoY changes, trend direction)

**Constraint:** Observation-level ONLY (no speculation or interpretation)

---

### Implications (I) — Impact

**Purpose:** Analyze consequences and business impact

**Content Focus:**
- Stakeholder impact analysis (who is affected, how)
- Confidence-stratified assertions
- Business and industry consequences

**Constraint:** Source from claim confidence scores; confidence-stratified assertions required

---

### Possibilities (P) — Options

**Purpose:** Explore future scenarios with explicit chance/risk framing

**Content Focus:**
- Cross-dimensional pattern synthesis
- **Chance scenarios** (value captured by acting)
- **Risk scenarios** (consequence of not acting)
- Forward-looking projections grounded in evidence

**Structured Format:**

| Sub-field | Guiding Question | Content |
|-----------|------------------|---------|
| Overview | "What could happen?" | General opportunity or scenario |
| Chance | "What do we gain by acting?" | Quantified upside, competitive advantage, value captured |
| Risk | "What do we lose by not acting?" | Cost of inaction, competitive loss, capability gap |

**Horizon-Specific Requirements:**

| Horizon | Chance/Risk | Quantification Level |
|---------|-------------|---------------------|
| **ACT** (0-2y) | **Required** | Specific metrics: $, %, timeframes |
| **PLAN** (2-5y) | Recommended | Directional projections, ranges |
| **OBSERVE** (5+y) | Optional | Qualitative, scenario-based |

**Constraint:** Only patterns present in loaded data; cross-dimensional synthesis allowed

---

### Solutions (S) — Action

**Purpose:** Provide actionable recommendations with digital implementation paths

**Content Focus:**
- High-confidence recommendations (≥0.75 only)
- Implementation guidance with timeframes
- Concrete next steps and success metrics
- **Business value quantification** from claims (e.g., "20% productivity increase")
- **Digital solution portfolio linkage** (when available)

**Constraint:** Implementation from domain-concepts only; high-confidence findings required

**Portfolio Integration (Optional):**

When a b2b-ict-portfolio research project is available:

- Link relevant portfolio entities that can implement the solution
- Explain specific business value the portfolio delivers
- Include quantified metrics where available from claims

---

## Usage Context

TIPS is the **content format** for trend entities within research frameworks. It defines the structural components, not the visual positioning or synthesis workflows.

**Research types that use TIPS:**

- `smarter-service` — TIPS structures each trend entity on the Trendradar
- Other research types may reference TIPS for trend-based synthesis

**Cross-Project Integration:**

- TIPS Solutions can reference `b2b-ict-portfolio` entities when available
- Portfolio linkage enables `customer-value-mapping` downstream processing
- Business value flows from claims → solutions → portfolio mapping
