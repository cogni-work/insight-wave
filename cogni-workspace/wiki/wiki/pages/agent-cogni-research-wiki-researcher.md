> One of the agents inside [[plugin-cogni-research]]. Model tier: **sonnet** — see [[concept-agent-model-strategy]].

Research a single sub-question by querying cogni-wiki instances. Reads wiki indexes to find relevant pages, extracts grounded findings from pre-synthesized wiki content. Never answers from model memory.
