> One of the skills inside [[plugin-cogni-consulting]].

Generate the final deliverable package for a Double Diamond engagement. Produces formatted outputs (PPTX, DOCX, XLSX, Excalidraw, themed HTML) by dispatching to cogni-visual and document-skills. The themed HTML deliverable combines all engagement content into one navigable document with concept diagrams and data charts via cogni-visual:enrich-report.
