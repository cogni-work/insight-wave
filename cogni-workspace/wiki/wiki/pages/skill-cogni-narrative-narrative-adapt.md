> One of the skills inside [[plugin-cogni-narrative]].

Transform existing narratives into derivative formats: executive briefs, talking points, and one-pagers. This skill should be used when the user asks to 'adapt a narrative', 'create executive brief', 'generate talking points', 'make a one-pager', 'shorten narrative', 'condense narrative', 'summarize the narrative', 'narrative to bullets', 'convert narrative to brief', 'prepare briefing notes', or when other plugins need derivative formats from a full narrative.
