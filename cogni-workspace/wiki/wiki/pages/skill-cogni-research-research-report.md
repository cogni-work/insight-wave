> One of the skills inside [[plugin-cogni-research]].

Generate a multi-agent research report using parallel web, local document, or wiki research with structural review. Three modes: basic (fast single-pass), detailed (multi-section with outline), deep (recursive tree exploration). Four source modes: web (default), local (user's files), wiki (cogni-wiki), hybrid. Requires an initialized project — routes to research-setup first if none exists. Runs localized search for the project's configured market (10 supported: DACH, DE, FR, IT, PL, NL, ES, US, UK, EU) with intent-based bilingual search and per-market authority sources.
