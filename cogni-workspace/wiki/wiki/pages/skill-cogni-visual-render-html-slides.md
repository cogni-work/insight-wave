> One of the skills inside [[plugin-cogni-visual]].

Render a presentation-brief.md (v4.0) into a self-contained HTML slide presentation with speaker notes toggle, keyboard navigation, themed styling, and Mermaid diagram support. After rendering, supports an interactive refinement loop where users can adjust individual slides — text fixes are applied directly to HTML, structural changes trigger a targeted re-render.
