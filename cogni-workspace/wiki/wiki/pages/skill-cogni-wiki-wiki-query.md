> One of the skills inside [[plugin-cogni-wiki]].

Answer a question by reading the Karpathy-style wiki — never from memory. Claude consults wiki/index.md first, then reads the relevant wiki/pages/*.md files, synthesizes an answer with "wikilink" double-bracket references citations, and optionally files the answer back as a new wiki page so the knowledge compounds.
