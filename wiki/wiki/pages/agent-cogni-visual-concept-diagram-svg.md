> One of the agents inside [[plugin-cogni-visual]]. Model tier: **sonnet** — see [[concept-agent-model-strategy]].

Generate a single concept diagram (TIPS flow, relationship map, process flow, or concept sketch) as clean inline SVG using LLM-crafted geometric primitives. No Excalidraw dependency. Produces gradient fills, drop shadows, zone backgrounds, and proper text anchoring. Callers provide data in, get SVG out.
