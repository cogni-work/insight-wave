> One of the skills inside [[plugin-cogni-visual]].

Review a visual brief from three stakeholder perspectives — design quality, audience experience, and usability. Supports all cogni-visual brief types: presentation-brief, web-brief, storyboard-brief, and infographic-brief. Returns a structured verdict (accept/revise/reject) with prioritized improvements.
