> One of the skills inside [[plugin-cogni-website]].

This skill plans the site structure for a cogni-website project interactively — discovering available content, proposing pages, mapping content to page sections, and generating website-plan.json. It should be triggered when the user mentions "plan the website", "site structure", "which pages", "website plan", "plan pages", "Seitenstruktur", "Website planen", "Seiten festlegen", "map content to pages", "which pages do I need", "welche Seiten brauche ich", or wants to decide what pages their website should have — even without saying "plan" explicitly.
