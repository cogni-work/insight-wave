It should be triggered when the user mentions "preview website", "open website", "Website anzeigen", "Website öffnen", "show me the website", "check the website", "validate links", "website preview", "how does it look", "wie sieht es aus", "test the website", "Website testen", or wants to see the generated site in a browser — even without saying "preview" explicitly.
